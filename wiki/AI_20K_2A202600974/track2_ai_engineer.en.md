# Track 2: AI Engineer (Data / Infra / LLMOps)


*Track 2 focuses on the **Data, Platform, and Operations (Engineering & Operations) Aspects** of an AI system. Learners in this Track play the role of an AI Engineer, Data Engineer, or MLOps Engineer - the person responsible for building data pipelines, optimizing vector databases, deploying models, and monitoring systems in the Production environment.*


*- **Data Engineering for AI**: Unstructured data processing, chunking, embedding, vector database optimization.*
*- **LLMOps & Monitoring**: Model lifecycle management, tracking latency, costs, and quality (Observability).*
*- **Fine-tuning & Alignment**: SFT, DPO, ORPO to align the model to specific domains (See also: [[concept_rag]]).*
*- **Infrastructure**: Kubernetes, FinOps, GPU optimization.*
*- **Data Governance**: Access management (RBAC), data security, PII filtering.*
