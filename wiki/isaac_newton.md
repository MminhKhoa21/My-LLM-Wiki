# Isaac Newton

**Sir Isaac Newton** (1642–1727) was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, alchemist, and theologian. He is widely recognized as one of the most influential scientists of all time.

## Contributions to Calculus

- **Method of Fluxions**: Newton developed his version of calculus, which he called the "method of fluxions", in the mid-1660s to describe the motion of planetary bodies and physical objects.
- **Concept of Fluxion**: A *fluxion* corresponds to what we now call the [[derivative]] (the rate of change of a quantity over time).
- **Co-invention Controversy**: Newton developed his calculus earlier than [[gottfried_leibniz]], but published it much later, leading to a bitter controversy over who invented calculus first. Today, both are acknowledged as independent co-inventors.
